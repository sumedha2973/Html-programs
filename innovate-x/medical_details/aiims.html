About AIIMS New Delhi

AIIMS New Delhi, established in 1956, is India's most prestigious medical institution known for its excellence in medical education, research, and patient care. It offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields of medicine and allied sciences. The institute is globally recognized for its high standards in clinical care and research innovation.
